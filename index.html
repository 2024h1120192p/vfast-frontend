About Us

VFAST
Hostel

The VFAST (visiting faculty and student transit) Hostel at BITS Pilani offers comfortable accommodation for visitors, faculty, and staff within the campus.


It features various types of rooms, including standard and deluxe double, and suite accommodations, equipped with amenities such as air conditioning, Wi-Fi, television, and laundry services. Guests can also enjoy meals in the dining area. Conveniently located within the campus, VFAST provides easy access to academic buildings, libraries, and other facilities. Reservations can be made through the online booking system.
